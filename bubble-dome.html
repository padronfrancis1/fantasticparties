Frequently Asked Questions

How many kids fit in the bubble dome?

Our large bubble dome comfortably accommodates up to 8 children, making it perfect for birthday parties and group celebrations. The small dome is designed for more intimate gatherings with 3-5 children. Both sizes provide enough space for kids to move around, play, and enjoy the balloons without feeling cramped. If you're unsure which size is right for your party, give us a call at 587-969-5441 and we'll help you choose based on your guest count and venue.

Can the dome be used outdoors?

Absolutely! Our bubble domes are completely weatherproof and work wonderfully for outdoor events in any season. Whether it's a summer garden party or a winter wonderland celebration, the dome protects your party from rain, snow, wind, and sun while maintaining visibility and natural light. We secure the dome properly to ensure it stays stable in Calgary's variable weather conditions. Just make sure you have a relatively flat surface and access to power for the inflation system.

Are balloons included?

Yes! Every bubble dome rental includes balloons inside the dome as part of your package. These balloons add a magical, festive atmosphere and are perfect for photos. If you'd like additional decorative balloons to match your party theme or colors, we can provide those for an extra cost. Just let us know your color preferences and theme when you book, and we'll help create the perfect look for your celebration.

How much space is needed?

The large dome requires approximately 15ft x 15ft of clear floor space, while the small dome needs about 10ft x 10ft. Both can be set up indoors or outdoors on a relatively flat surface. You'll also need access to a standard power outlet within reasonable distance to keep the dome inflated throughout your event. For indoor setups, make sure you have adequate ceiling height. We recommend measuring your space before booking, and we're happy to discuss specific venue requirements when you call.

Can we decorate the dome?

Yes, you're welcome to add your own decorations to personalize the bubble dome! The included balloons provide a great foundation, and many customers add string lights, banners, themed props, or additional balloon arrangements to match their party aesthetic. We recommend avoiding anything with sharp edges or adhesives that might damage the dome material. Feel free to get creative - our bubble domes are the perfect canvas for your party vision!
