Why Choose Our Soft Play for Your Calgary Party?

Looking for safe, engaging entertainment for your toddler's party? Our soft play equipment is specifically designed for young children ages 1-4, with safety and developmental benefits at the forefront. Unlike hard plastic toys or inflatables that can be intimidating for little ones, our foam-based soft play set provides a gentle, confidence-building environment where crawlers and early walkers can explore, climb, and play without worry.

Every piece in our soft play collection features soft edges and non-toxic materials, ensuring complete peace of mind for parents. The beautiful pastel colors in green, pink, blue, and yellow create a visually stimulating yet calming environment that toddlers love. The varied textures and shapes help develop motor skills, spatial awareness, and coordination while keeping little ones engaged and entertained throughout your event.

Our soft play equipment is perfect for Calgary's indoor party needs, especially during long winter months when outdoor play isn't an option. Whether you're hosting a first birthday party at home, a daycare celebration, or a baby-friendly family gathering, our complete setup includes foam climbing blocks, a soft slide, ball pit with colorful balls, climbing structures, and a cozy playhouse. We handle all delivery, setup, and takedown, so you can focus on enjoying precious moments with your little one.

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages is soft play suitable for?

Our soft play equipment is ideal for toddlers and young children ages 1-4 years old. It's specifically designed to be safe for crawlers and early walkers, with soft edges and age-appropriate climbing heights. The gentle nature of the foam equipment makes it perfect for building confidence in little ones who are just discovering their physical abilities.

Is the equipment safe and non-toxic?

Absolutely! Safety is our top priority. All foam pieces are made from non-toxic, child-safe materials with soft edges and no sharp corners. The equipment is regularly cleaned and sanitized between rentals. The soft construction ensures that even if children bump into pieces or take a tumble, they remain safe and protected.

How much space is needed?

Our soft play setup requires approximately 10ft x 10ft of floor space, making it perfect for living rooms, basements, or community halls. The equipment works great on carpet, hardwood, or any flat indoor surface. We'll work with you to customize the arrangement based on your available space and ensure the best play experience for the children.

Can soft play be combined with other rentals?

Yes! Soft play pairs perfectly with our other toddler-friendly rentals. Many customers combine it with our bubble domes for a magical play experience, or add our kids' chairs and tables for a complete party setup. We can create custom packages that include multiple items to make your party planning easier. Contact us at 587-969-5441 to discuss combination packages.

How do I book and what does it cost?

Booking is easy! Simply call us at 587-969-5441 or use our contact form. Pricing varies based on the specific items you need and your location in Calgary. We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance, especially for weekend parties. We'll provide a custom quote based on your party needs and ensure availability for your preferred date.
