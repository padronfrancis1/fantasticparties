Frequently Asked Questions

What ages are the chairs and tables suitable for?

Our kids party chairs and tables are specifically designed for children ages 1-7 years old. The 55cm chair height and 45cm table height are perfectly proportioned for this age range, allowing children to sit comfortably with their feet touching the ground and easy access to the table surface. Younger toddlers can use them with supervision, while children up to age 7 will find them comfortable and appropriately sized.

Is a deposit required?

No, we do not require any deposit for our chairs and tables rental! We want to make it as easy and affordable as possible for Calgary families to host amazing parties. Simply pay the rental fee ($3 per chair, $4 per table per day) when you pick up or receive delivery. This no-deposit policy reflects our commitment to hassle-free party planning.

Can I pick up instead of having delivery?

Absolutely! We offer free pickup from our Evergreen SW Calgary location. This is a great option if you have a vehicle that can accommodate the chairs and tables you need, and it saves you money on delivery fees. Just schedule a pickup time when you book, and we'll have everything clean and ready for you to load. Many customers prefer this option for smaller orders.

How many chairs and tables should I order?

A good rule of thumb is to order 1 chair per child attending the party. For tables, we recommend 1 table for every 4 children, as our tables comfortably seat 4 kids. For example, a party with 12 children would need 12 chairs and 3 tables. If you're planning activities like crafts or meals where all kids need simultaneous seating, make sure to have enough furniture for everyone. Contact us if you need help calculating the right quantity!

Can they be used outdoors?

Yes! Our kids party chairs and tables are suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. They're made from durable, weather-resistant plastic that can handle Calgary's varying conditions. They're perfect for backyard birthday parties during summer months or indoor events when the weather isn't cooperating. The chairs and tables are easy to wipe clean, making them practical for outdoor use where kids might get a bit messy.
