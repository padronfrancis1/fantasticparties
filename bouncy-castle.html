Frequently Asked Questions

What ages can use the bouncy castle?

Our commercial-grade bouncy castle is designed for all ages, from toddlers taking their first bouncy steps to older kids and even teens. The spacious 9ft x 9ft interior provides enough room for multiple age groups to enjoy bouncing together safely. We do recommend separating very young toddlers from older, more energetic children during use to ensure everyone can play comfortably at their own pace.

Is the bouncy castle safe?

Absolutely! Safety is our top priority. Our bouncy castle features commercial-grade PVC construction with reinforced seams, safety netting on all four sides to prevent falls, and a continuous airflow blower that maintains proper inflation throughout your event. We use professional anchoring systems to secure the castle whether indoors or outdoors. Adult supervision is always required, and we provide safety guidelines with every rental to ensure proper use.

Can it be used indoors?

Yes! Our bouncy castle works great for indoor use, which is perfect for Calgary's unpredictable weather. You'll need a space of at least 10ft x 10ft for the footprint, plus adequate ceiling clearance of at least 8ft (preferably higher). The venue should have access to a standard electrical outlet for the blower. We commonly set up in basements, garages, community centers, school gyms, and church halls. Contact us to discuss your specific indoor venue and we'll confirm if it's suitable.

What if it rains?

If you've booked the bouncy castle for outdoor use and weather becomes a concern, we have options! If your venue has indoor space available, we can set up inside instead (space permitting). Alternatively, we're happy to work with you to reschedule your rental for a better weather day at no additional charge. We monitor Calgary weather forecasts closely and will reach out proactively if we see potential issues with your scheduled date. Light drizzle won't affect the castle, but we won't operate in heavy rain, lightning, or high winds for safety reasons.

How do I book and what does it cost?

To book our bouncy castle, simply call us at 403-918-5507 or fill out our contact form with your event date, location, and details. Pricing varies based on rental duration, delivery distance within Calgary, and specific event needs, so we provide custom quotes for each booking. We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance, especially during peak party season (spring and summer), to ensure availability for your preferred date. Our team will walk you through the entire process and answer any questions you have.
