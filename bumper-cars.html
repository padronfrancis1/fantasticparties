Frequently Asked Questions

How many kids can ride the bumper cars at once?

Our standard package includes 4 bumper cars, so up to 4 children can ride at the same time. If you're expecting more kids or want to reduce wait times, you can add additional bumper cars for $100 each. This allows more children to play simultaneously and keeps the party moving smoothly.

What's the weight/age limit for bumper cars?

Our bumper cars are designed for children ages 1-6 years old. Each car has a weight limit to ensure safe operation. For specific weight restrictions or if you have questions about whether the bumper cars are suitable for your child, please contact us at 587-969-5441 and we'll be happy to provide detailed specifications.

Can bumper cars be used indoors?

Yes! Our bumper cars are perfect for indoor use, making them ideal for Calgary's unpredictable weather. You'll need a minimum space of 15ft x 15ft (the arena is 13ft x 13ft) and access to a standard power outlet. We can set up in basements, garages, community centers, or any indoor venue with adequate space and flooring.

Do parents need to supervise?

Yes, adult supervision is required at all times during bumper car use. We provide parent remote controls that allow you to manage speed and stop individual cars if needed. This ensures maximum safety while giving kids the freedom to enjoy themselves. Parents can easily supervise from the sidelines while maintaining full control.

How long does setup take?

Our experienced team typically takes 30-45 minutes to complete the full setup, including inflating the arena, positioning the bumper cars, and running safety checks. We recommend booking us to arrive about an hour before your party starts to ensure everything is ready when your guests arrive. Takedown is equally quick and efficient.
