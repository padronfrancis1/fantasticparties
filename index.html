Frequently Asked Questions

What ages are your party rentals suitable for?

Our bumper cars are designed for kids aged 1-6 years old with adjustable speed settings and parent remote control. Bubble domes, bouncy castles, and soft play equipment are suitable for kids of all ages. Party chairs and tables fit children aged 1-7 years old.

Do you deliver and set up the party equipment?

Yes! We handle all setup and takedown for bumper cars, bubble domes, bouncy castles, and soft play equipment in Calgary. Setup and delivery fees vary by location. For chairs and tables, you can pick up in Evergreen, SW Calgary or request delivery for an extra charge.

How much do your party rentals cost in Calgary?

Our bumper cars package (4 cars + inflatable arena) starts at $350 for 2-3 hours. Bubble domes range from $180 (small) to $250 (large) for 3-4 hours. Kids party chairs are $3 each/day and tables are $4 each/day. Contact us for bouncy castle and soft play pricing!

What areas in Calgary do you serve?

We serve all areas of Calgary and surrounding communities. Delivery fees vary depending on your location. Call us at 587-969-5441 for a quote!

How do I book a party rental?

Booking is easy! Call us at 587-969-5441, email fantasticpartiesyyc@gmail.com, or reach out through our Facebook or Instagram pages. We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for your preferred date.
